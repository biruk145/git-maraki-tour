Founder

Founded in 2018 G.C by tour operator Asmarew Mola, Mekdela Tours specializes in organizing custom tours to Ethiopia’s most remarkable destinations.
Asmarew is a native Ethiopian who was born and raised in Gondar, a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its 11 monolithic rock-hewn churches. He later moved to Addis Ababa, another UNESCO-registered historical city, to study Batchlor.
Driven by his passion for Ethiopian heritage and travel. Today, he stands among the leading tour operators in Addis Ababa, offering a wide range of personalized tours across the country, from cultural and historical experiences to nature and adventure-focused journeys.
